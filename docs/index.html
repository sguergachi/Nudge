01
Sensor Fusion

Harvest Engine

Samples your foreground app, idle time & browser domain at 1 Hz. Every reading graded for signal quality.

Rolling 300-second window computes switch velocity, distinct-app count, per-app time share, and anchor-return events.

Three raw signals fused into a 26-dimension feature vector — the full snapshot the AI evaluates every 60 seconds.

Cross-platform · Windows & Linux · Wayland & X11
02
Auto-Classification

App Categories

Every app and browser domain is classified into 6 categories, not just a raw process name.

5 confidence tiers — from OS-installed metadata (ground truth) down to keyword matching and sensible defaults.

Browser tabs inherit the anchor app's category — research on documentation doesn't get flagged as distraction.

Development · Creative · Office · Communication · Entertainment · Utility
03
Inference Engine

AI Brain

Trained exclusively on your YES/NO responses — not a generic model from thousands of other users.

Compares every 26-dimension snapshot against patterns it has seen before. Sub-100ms inference.

Activates after 100 training samples. Retrains automatically at every 50 new responses.

Personal model · No cloud · Automatic retraining
04
Living Dashboard

Analytics

Track your focus across Today, This Week, This Month, and All Time views.

See hourly productivity breakdowns, most-used apps ranked by active time, and per-app category colors.

The AI Brain tab exposes prediction history, confidence sparklines, sensor signal quality, and model training statistics.

Access from system tray · 4 time windows · ML diagnostics

Adaptive Interrupt

Check-in

No fixed schedule — fires only when drift confidence hits ≥98%. Stays silent when you're in flow.

30-second auto-dismiss if you're busy. Every answer feeds back to sharpen the model.

≥98% confidence · 30s dismiss · Keyboard-first
