Harvest Engine

Sensor fusion that sees your focus.

Three signals. One privacy-respecting pipeline. 21 features.

  • App focus — what you're running
  • Idle time — seconds since input
  • Browser domain — what site you're on
Smart Categorisation

Knows the difference between coding and scrolling.

Every app and browser domain is classified into one of six categories. Browser tabs inherit the anchor app — so reading docs doesn't look like a distraction.

  • 6 categories — Development, Creative, Office, Communication, Entertainment, Utility
  • 5-tier confidence — from OS ground truth down to sensible defaults
  • Browser inheritance — tabs inherit anchor app's category
AI Brain

Learns you. Not people like you.

Your model. Your patterns. Your YES/NO answers. It compares every 21-feature snapshot against what it has seen before — then decides.

  • Drift detected — triggers a check-in
  • Productive rhythm — stays silent
  • Every response — sharpens the next prediction
Check-in & Settings

How it works

Sense → Fuse → Predict → Nudge

A four-stage pipeline that runs entirely on your machine — from raw desktop signals to a split-second YES/NO check-in. No cloud, no telemetry, no pointless fixed timers.

1

Sense

Your active app, idle time, and browser domain.
No screenshots. No keystrokes. No cloud.

2

Classify

Every app gets a category — Development, Creative, Office, Communication, Entertainment, or Utility. 5 confidence tiers, from OS metadata down to defaults.

3

Fuse & Predict

Category + idle + switching + anchor return + domain = 21-feature snapshot. The AI compares it to your past patterns and decides: drift or focused?

4

Nudge

"Still focused?"
YES / NO. Keyboard shortcuts. Auto-dismiss. Every answer feeds back and sharpens the model.
