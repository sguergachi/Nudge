Check-in notification

A gentle nudge when it matters

When Nudge detects you might be drifting, a sleek dark window appears with a simple YES/NO choice. Keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Shift+Y / Ctrl+Shift+N) let you answer without touching the mouse.

  • 30-second auto-dismiss with countdown ring
  • Drag to reposition — position is remembered
  • Subtle acrylic blur effect blends with your desktop
Analytics dashboard

Understand your focus patterns

The analytics window surfaces your productivity data across three tabs: Activity, Timeline, and AI Brain. See at a glance how you spent your day and where your focus peaks.

  • Real-time stat cards: activity time, productive %, apps tracked
  • Hourly productivity breakdown with green/red bar charts
  • AI prediction history with confidence sparklines
Settings

Fine-tune to your workflow

Control every aspect of Nudge from the settings window — toggle ML, adjust check intervals, manage notifications, and monitor your AI model's training status.

  • Toggle on-device ML on/off at any time
  • Customizable check interval (5–15 min range)
  • Live model stats: samples collected, accuracy, last trained
How it works

Track. Learn. Nudge.

Three simple steps to reclaim your focus, powered by an AI that runs entirely on your machine.

1

Track silently

Nudge monitors your foreground app and idle time — no screenshots, no keystroke logging, no cloud. Just a lightweight daemon running in the background.

2

Train the model

Each time you answer "productive?" the on-device model learns. After ~50 responses it starts predicting drift with over 80% accuracy.

3

Get nudged

When the AI detects you're drifting, a quick check-in appears. When you're focused it stays silent. No noise, no fixed timers, no distractions.
