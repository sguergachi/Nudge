How it works

01

Sensor Fusion Engine

  • Harvest engine samples foreground app, idle time & browser domain at 1 Hz
  • Rolling 300 s window captures switch velocity, distinct-app count & anchor-return events
  • Every app classified into one of 6 categories at 5 confidence tiers
  • Three raw signals fused into a 26-dimension feature vector
Sources: KWin Script · X11 EWMH · Sway IPC · Win32 · browser domain extraction

02

On-device AI Model

  • Personal scikit-learn binary classifier — your model, trained on your responses
  • Inference over TCP 127.0.0.1:45002 at sub-100ms latency
  • Activates after 100 training samples; background trainer retriggers at every 50 new responses
  • Adaptive architecture: lightweight (<200 samples), standard (200+)
RandomForest / LogisticRegression · sub-100ms · retrains automatically · no cloud
03

Adaptive Interrupt & Live Analytics

  • No fixed schedule — the model fires only when drift confidence reaches ≥98%
  • Stays silent when you're productive; falls back to variable-interval (5–10 min) below threshold
  • Ctrl+Shift+Y / Ctrl+Shift+N respond without leaving the keyboard
  • 30s auto-dismiss · every answer feeds back and sharpens the next prediction
≥98% confidence · 30s auto-dismiss · keyboard shortcuts · UDP 127.0.0.1:45001
Live analytics dashboard

Track your focus trends from the system tray. Daily, weekly, and monthly breakdowns of productive vs. distracted time. Every YES/NO you give builds your personal focus history — visible anytime from the tray menu.
