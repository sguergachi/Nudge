Harvest Engine

Signal fusion that understands your workflow

The Nudge Harvest Engine is a V2 sensor fusion pipeline that builds a rich ActivityContext every tick. It fuses signals from your focus source (KWin Script on KDE/Wayland, X11 EWMH, Sway IPC, or Win32), signal quality assessment, browser domain extraction, and rolling 300-second windows to produce 21 ML features.

  • Multi-source focus detection — KWin, X11, Sway, Wayland, Win32
  • Signal quality scores: Trusted, Usable, Poor
  • Rolling window analytics: switch counts, distinct apps, app share, anchor return
AI Brain

A model tailored to you — personally

The AI Brain is an on-device scikit-learn model that trains exclusively on your data. Each time you answer a check-in, it learns your unique focus patterns — which apps help you concentrate, what times of day you're most productive, and when you typically drift off task.

  • Reaches ~80% accuracy after just 50+ labeled samples
  • Interrupts only when confidence exceeds 98% — no false alarms
  • Background trainer retrains automatically as new samples arrive
Full control

A gentle nudge when it matters. Settings when you need them.

When the AI detects you're drifting, a sleek dark check-in window appears with a simple YES/NO choice. Keyboard shortcuts let you answer without touching the mouse. The settings window gives you full control over ML, notifications, check intervals, and model stats.

  • 30-second auto-dismiss with countdown ring + drag-to-reposition
  • Toggle ML on/off, customize check interval, manage notification sounds
  • Live model stats: samples collected, training accuracy, last trained timestamp
How it works

Track. Learn. Nudge.

Three simple steps to reclaim your focus, powered by an AI that runs entirely on your machine.

1

Track with the Harvest Engine

The Nudge Harvest Engine silently fuses signals from your foreground app, idle time, and browser domains — no screenshots, no keystroke logging, no cloud.

2

Train the AI Brain

Each time you answer a check-in, the on-device model learns your unique focus patterns. After ~50 responses it starts predicting drift with over 80% accuracy.

3

Get nudged

When the AI Brain detects you're drifting with high confidence, a quick check-in appears. When you're focused it stays silent. No noise, no fixed timers.
