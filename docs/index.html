Harvest Engine

What's running on your machine. In real time.

The Nudge Harvest Engine is the sensing layer — it silently observes what's in focus on your desktop and how you're interacting with it. Every tick, it captures which application is active, how long you've been there, whether you're idle, and what domain you're looking at if you're in a browser. It doesn't record your screen, log your keystrokes, or send anything to the cloud. It simply builds a live picture of your attention — what you're doing, for how long, and whether you're engaged or coasting.

  • Observes foreground app, idle time, and browser domains — nothing else
  • Grades every reading by reliability so the model knows when to trust it
  • Tracks patterns across a rolling window — switch frequency, time per app, return-to-anchor behaviour
AI Brain

Learns you. Not people like you.

The AI Brain is a model built exclusively from your YES/NO answers — it has never seen another person's data. Every time you respond to a check-in, the Brain maps what you were doing at that moment (which app, how long you'd been there, how many times you'd switched recently) against whether you said focused or drifting. Over time it builds a precise picture of your personal focus patterns: the apps where you're always locked in, the hours when you tend to fade, the domains that pull you off course. It won't nudge you unless it's genuinely confident you've drifted.

  • Becomes reliable after about 50 responses — tailored to your specific workflow
  • Only triggers when conviction is high — no annoying false alarms
  • Sharpens automatically over time as you provide more feedback
Full control

A gentle nudge when it matters. Full control when you need it.

When the AI Brain detects you're drifting, a small check-in window appears with a simple question: still focused? Two buttons — YES and NO. Keyboard shortcuts let you answer without touching the mouse. If you don't respond, the window quietly dismisses itself. The settings panel gives you full command: turn the AI on or off, set how often you want to be checked, manage sound preferences, and see how the model is doing — how many samples it's collected and how accurate it's become at reading your patterns.

  • Fades in when it matters, dismisses silently if you're busy
  • Answer with a click or a keyboard shortcut — your choice
  • Toggle AI, adjust check frequency, monitor model accuracy — all from one panel
How it works

Sense → Fuse → Predict → Nudge

A four-stage pipeline that runs entirely on your machine — from raw desktop signals to a split-second YES/NO check-in. No cloud, no telemetry, no pointless fixed timers.

1

Sense

The Harvest Engine continuously observes what's on your screen — which application is active, how long you've been there, whether you've stepped away, and what website you're on if you're in a browser. Every observation is tagged with a confidence score so the system knows how reliable it is. No screenshots, no recordings, no keystroke logs — just the name of what's in focus.

2

Fuse

Raw observations are combined into a single picture of your attention. The fusion layer tracks how often you switch between apps, how long you spend in each one, whether you keep returning to the same tool, and how your idle time relates to your work rhythm. Browser domains are separated from general app names so an afternoon on documentation reads differently from an afternoon on social media. All of this is distilled into a concise snapshot of what focused looks like for you.

3

Predict

The AI Brain evaluates that snapshot against everything it has learned from your previous YES/NO responses. If it detects the same pattern it has seen before when you said you were drifting, and it's highly confident in that assessment, it signals a check-in. If it's uncertain or it recognises a productive rhythm, it stays quiet — no dings, no popups, no false alarms. The Brain gets more accurate with every answer you give it.

4

Nudge

A small, unobtrusive window appears and asks a simple question: still focused? YES keeps you moving, NO logs the drift. If you're in the middle of something, keyboard shortcuts let you answer without leaving your flow. Leave it unanswered and it quietly disappears on its own. Every response feeds back into the model, making the next prediction sharper. Over time, Nudge learns exactly when to speak up and when to stay silent.
